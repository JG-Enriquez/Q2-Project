Kawasan Falls

A beautiful sight in the Philippines! With's it's clear turquoise water and natural cave formation, it's a perfect resting sight for vacations. Take your friends with you and have some fun in the water.


Cebu, Visayas

Boracay Beach

A beach built for relaxation, one of Philippine's most treasured locations. The sand is bright and soft and the sea is a bright blue. If you want to spend your vacay somewhere, this is a place to be!


Aklan, Visayas

Tubbataha Reef

A coral reef known for it's marine life and beautiful underwater scaping. Here you can find a variety of sea creatures and even swim along them. Each sight here is a sight worth to see!


Palawan, Luzon

Intramuros

A structure built during the colonial period of the Philippines. It is considered a national landmark and takes is heavily inspired by Spanish structure and architecture.


Manila, Luzon

Rice Terraces

A beautiful land of agriculture, cultivated by the people of the Philippines and preserved over time-- A contribution given to us by our ancestors.


Banaue, Luzon

Chocolate Hills

A natural land formation of our land, known for it's change in color over the seasons. It's most well-known color being a chocolate brown, which is the reason for it's name.
